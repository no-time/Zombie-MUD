# Zombie MUD 🧟‍♂️

Welcome to **Zombie MUD**, a deeply mechanical, text-based rogue-lite survival RPG built entirely in PowerShell. Navigate a post-apocalyptic nightmare, fight mutated horrors, build your character, and pass on your legacy to future survivors when you inevitably perish.

## 🌟 Key Features

* **12 Distinct Classes:** Play as a hulking *Bruiser*, a charismatic *Popular* survivor, an ammo-hoarding *Immune Human*, or even unlock the occult *Vampire*. Each class features wildly different playstyles, resource pools (SP, BP, Ammo), and completely unique unlockable mechanics at Level 10 and 20.
* **Deep Combat Engine:** A turn-based tactical battle system featuring elemental procs, DoTs (Bleed, Zombie Rot), crowd control (Stun, Flinch, Tumble), stealth mechanics, dual-wielding, and "Miracle Dodges" driven by a robust Luck stat.
* **Pet & Companion System:** Why fight alone? Use infectious diseases or specific class skills to convert defeated enemies into loyal pets that level up alongside you, dealing damage, healing you, or granting aura buffs.
* **Rogue-lite Progression:** Death is only a setback.
  * **Account Legacy:** Reaching level milestones permanently buffs every future character you create.
  * **The Stash:** A safe box in the starting room to pass powerful weapons and armor to your next life.
  * **Ouroboros System:** If you die, your zombified corpse drops your gear in the exact room you perished. Return to that room in your next life to fight your old self and reclaim your artifacts!
* **Elemental Upgrades & Crafting:** Collect elements like Bonechips and Gunpowder to permanently mutate your base stats. Use the Technologist's *Mad Inventor* skill to fuse duplicate items into god-tier equipment.
